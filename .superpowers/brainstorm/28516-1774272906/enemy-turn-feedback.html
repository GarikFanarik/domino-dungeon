Persistent log panel below the battlefield. Events stream in as they happen. Always visible, no interruption to flow.

Animated Sequence

Enemy stone briefly shown → damage number flies. Cinematic, no click needed, automatically clears before your turn.

Turn Summary Popup

Card overlay with full breakdown after turn resolves. Most readable, but requires a click to dismiss.
